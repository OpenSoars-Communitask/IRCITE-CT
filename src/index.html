Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is CommuniTask?

CommuniTask is a mobile application that bridges the gap of connecting individuals in the Philippines with micro job opportunities and fostering inclusivity. CommuniTask provides a digital platform intended to connect those people in need of service and at service. Read More

2. Who developed CommuniTask?

A group of IT students specializing in Web and Mobile Applications Development from Bulacan State University Main Campus namely Ryka Gene Austria, Mary Queen Casaclang, Richard James Bagay, Janniel Andrei De Jesus, and Jeaysmie Digo.Read More

3. How does CommuniTask work?

Hire a service you want and apply to a service you know you can provide. Our platform provides a user-friendly interface for both hiring the services you desire and applying your skills to fulfill the needs of others. Join us today to experience the convenience and efficiency of our dynamic service marketplace.Read More

4. Who are the partners of CommuniTask?

CommuniTask has established partnerships with the Development Academy of the Philippines (DAP) and secured initial funding for its development. It has also teamed up with the Malolos City LGU as an early adopter, along with the Bulacan State University (BulSU). Furthermore, CommuniTask is currently incubated under BARAS Technology Business Incubator (BARAS TBI).Read More

5. What are the unique features of CommuniTask?

In addition to the core functionalities of hiring and applying for services, we've integrated a map feature enabling users to track the routes of our mobile vendors. Furthermore, our platform includes in-app messaging for seamless communication among users. Lastly, we offer a variety of courses to empower users to learn and diversify their skill sets.Read More

6. How can we make sure we do not get scammed?

CommuniTask employs a facial verification feature to authenticate users, necessitating the upload of identification documents. As an early adopter in Malolos City, users may also furnish barangay certificates as additional proof of identity.Read More

7. What would be its source of revenue?

Revenue will be generated via commissions charged to users who utilize the services offered on the platform.

8. Could CommuniTask potentially integrate blockchain technology?

Yes, integrating blockchain into CommuniTask is feasible. Points could serve as an alternative method for users to earn income through Bitcoin transactions.

About

CommuniTask is a mobile application currently under development by 4th year IT students from Bulacan State University. CommuniTask focuses on bridging the bridges the gap of connecting individuals in the Philippines with micro job opportunities. CommuniTask recently won the National START Hachathon 2023 hosted by Development Academy of the Philippines (DAP) and provided a seed funding for its development. CommuniTask is incubating under DOST BARAS-TBI and to be adapted by Malolos City LGU for utilization. CommuniTask aims to expand its reach to additional cities, fostering inclusivity and creating greater opportunities for all.
